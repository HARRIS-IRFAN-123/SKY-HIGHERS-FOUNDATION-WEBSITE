ABOUT SKY HIGHERS FOUNDATION

Since 2011, Sky Highers Foundation has been working for Poverty Alleviation, providing social welfare services in Faisalabad on a non-political, non-commercial, and non-communal basis, serving without any discrimination of caste, color, class, and creed. SHF is a non-profit organization under registration no. RJSC/FSD/2112 in Pakistan.

Saad Ijaz, the founder of Sky Highers Foundation, had a dream of serving humanity. After losing his father in his childhood, his heart developed compassion for every orphan and deserving people. He saw himself in every orphan. A passion to work for people arose in his heart. However, he belonged to a middle-class family, but it was not all that certain for him. He kept moving for people and embellished their lives. He endured many hardships in this war but continued to walk in the name of Allah. What We Are Doing?

SHF imbued with a noble mission to provide relief to the suffering humanity through various projects like Food Project, Electric water cooler project, Grocery Project, wheel chair/ tricycle project, Dowry Project, Ishal-e-sawab Project, Education project, Rozgar Project, Kafan project, and other relief services.PROJECTS

Our Aim

Sky Highers Foundation was founded on the principle of Helping Hand. Our aim is to break the cycle of poverty, mitigating the financial troubles of the poor, giving people a chance to live a dignified life.

Our Goal

Our goal is to empower people by helping them in their education and providing them skills and trained them for their better future lives.
